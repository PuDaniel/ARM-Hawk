Stock material: Aluminium Square profile 16x16x26mm
Vertically mounted in vise.
Check clearance for 16mm milling depth.
Zero (X,Y) at center point of square.

1: 	tip_bottom
	3mm cutter (with min 16mm teeth length)
	Z=0 at top face of stock

2:	tip_predrill
	3mm countersink
	Z=0 at top face of stock (tip of tool)

3:	tip_drill
	2mm drill
	Z=0.5 at top face of stock (tip of tool)

Cut raw end of workpiece to a total length of 14.5mm.
Turn workpiece around (180deg, holes downwards) and mount in vise.
Zero (X,Y) at axis of previously milled cylinder.

4:	tip_top
	3mm finger cutter
	Z=0.5 (bottom tip of cutter) at top face of cylinder

5:	3mm countersink
	Z=0 at topmost point of sphere (tip of tool)
	sink 0.5mm deep at (X,Y)=(0,0) (S5000 F30)

6:	1mm drill
	Z=0.3 at topmost point of sphere (tip of tool)
	drill 3mm deep at (X,Y)=(0,0) (S10000 F10)

clamp down 1.5mm aluminium sheet with min 80x30mm (X,Y)
Zero (X,Y) min 5mm from left/lower border
	
7:	angle_base
	3mm cutter
	Z=0 at top of sheet metal

Mount tip with help of angle base at the right angle (45deg) into vise.
Zero Y at cylinder axis; zero X -6mm offset from farthest point of sphere.

8:	3mm countersink
	Z=0 at topmost point of sphere (tip of tool)
	sink 0.5mm deep at (X,Y)=(0,0) (S5000 F30)

9:	1mm drill
	Z=0.3 at topmost point of sphere (tip of tool)
	drill 1.3mm deep at (X,Y)=(0,0) (S10000 F10)

Rotate cylinder axis 90deg and repeat steps 8-9 3 times (four holes).
